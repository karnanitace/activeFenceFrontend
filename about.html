ActiveFence identifies and tracks malicious activities online, at scale. By scanning—in hidden sources of chatter and across the web—bad actors, the content they share and the networks they operate, we protect billions of people worldwide from violent extremism, disinformation, child sexual abuse, fraud and other harms on the internet. We cover thousands of sources in over 20 languages.

Our Story

ActiveFence was founded to fight evil online and make the internet a safer place. Our technology was originally built to combat ISIS and other violent extremism online, at scale. Through our work with some of the world’s largest technology platforms in this area, we realized that our technology could also support growing needs in other abuse areas.

As parents and as concerned citizens, our founders worked to quickly grow the types of areas we cover to include countering terror, human trafficking, child predator activity, disinformation and brand smear campaigns online.

Today, we support Fortune 500 technology companies, brands, NGOs, and government agencies in their quest to identify, track, and prevent online evil at scale.
