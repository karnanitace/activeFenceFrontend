The Challenge

Increasingly sophisticated actors engage in various fraudulent behaviors that affect platforms and brands. These include creating fake accounts, reviews and likes, conducting refund, trial,... coupon, chargeback or gift card abuse, and buying and selling compromised accounts.

Read more

Our Solution

ActiveFence scans—at scale—the sources where fraudsters are active to understand what is being bought and sold in closed forums and the dark web to support platforms as they protect... individual users, update product strategy and work to stop bad actor operations.

Read more

Key Benefits

  • Understand which accounts or users have been compromised for one-to-one customer security and communication actions.

  • Stop entire fraudulent operations — track bad actors selling accounts or reviews in order to keep them off platform and treat them with cease and desist protocols.

  • Understand the means and methods of fraudsters who abuse your platform— inform product, security and AI/ ML roadmaps based on their evolving methods.

  • Test the effectiveness of new platform security features “in the wild” and understand how bad actors can work around them. features “in the wild” and understand how bad actors can work around them.
